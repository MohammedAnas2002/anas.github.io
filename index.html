About Me

I'm a PhD candidate at Savitribai Phule Pune University specializing in the intersection of artificial intelligence and seismic engineering. My research focuses on developing hybrid systems that combine physics-based models with deep learning to predict and mitigate earthquake risks in urban environments.

With experience at Microsoft Research, Tata Consultancy Services, and National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, I've developed expertise in machine learning, computer vision, and resilient infrastructure design. My work aims to bridge the gap between theoretical AI research and practical applications in disaster prevention.
